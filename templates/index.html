What is CARPY?

CARPY is a tool for testing enrichment of protein properties.

Currently 40+ properties compiled from SGD and high-throughput analyses are tracked for over 6000 proteins.

The database can be queried by SGID or yeast ORF identifiers

How does it work?

The input for CARPY is a list of protein IDs or gene names

CARPY performs statistical analysis of all properties to find those that are significantly enriched or de-enriched in your dataset compared to the proteome

More information on the statistical analysis can be found on the about page
